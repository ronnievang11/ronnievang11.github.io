Web Hosting

Researching for a free hosting website and a paid hosting website is not that easy for me. Although, I do have a fun time searching about it because you really cannot trust a website saying it is free, but then ended up with a price fee on their website. While searching for free hosting website, I came across byethost.com. There is plenty of free website than I thought; however, ByetHost caught my attention the most. ByetHost is very reliable and they have a great service. They are well known for PHP, mySQL, PHPbb, SMF, Wiki, and free subdomain. If you want to get more of their offerings, you can also do a monthly fee for their premium hosting plans, business hosting plans, and so on. They made it very easy for their customer to sign up. They have different type of free hosting you can select for whatever you plan on host. Here is where you can find more basic information (Links to an external site.)of the free hosting consists. When I research for a paid hosting website, I came to ipage.com. iPage is a paid hosting website which costs about $1.89 per month for a newly sign up user. Their renewal price is range to $8.99/mo. iPage is known to be the top ranking for paid hosting website. iPage has an essential plan which is very beneficial to their users. They provide a full featured website include 2 advanced drag and drop website builder tools, online store, blog, photo gallery, security suite, and etc. They are also known to be one of the cheapest and very affordable. However, they only allow a 30 days money-back. Overall, compare to other paid hosting website out there, they are reliable and also have a strong support plan.

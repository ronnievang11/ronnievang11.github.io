The dark net is a very interesting place. The most important part about the dark place is it’s creative, secure, and difficult to censor. Best thing of all, is that no one will have to ever know about your identity. Not only that, there is no pop up or ads. As well as there is so many things you can search and get than what you will hardly find searching on the web. Honestly, I really am interesting in the dark net. If I am able to get a chance to visit the dark net, I will want to check it out and experience what it is like. After watching this video, I now realize that getting in the dark net is not as easy as it looks. The dark net uses a different and special browser such as the Tor browser. Not only that, everything has to be confidential like your mailing address and changing your currency to bit coin. What fascinates me was how there is such an open source browser like Tor that allows anyone to browse the net without giving away their location by encrypting your IP address and routing them to several other computers around the world. I think we will eventually going to the dark net, especially through a chain of having someone joining and bringing more to join. I also believe that dark net will be better known because we personally are becoming worrying about our privacy by spending most of our time being on the web.
